Befiler Details

This is an example of portfolio details

• Befiler is the quickest and simplest way to get NTN and File your taxes with complete support from their tax expert, making the process effortless.

• Was assigned to lead the team of front-end developers and deliver features like migration, refactoring, payment integration, and Auth 2.0.

• The product was initially built on AngularJS, we had to write every module from scratch to migrate it to Angular(Angular2+) and bootstrap.

• Parallel to this we also delivered some features in the old repository. To manage the project using agile we used Jira and Bitbucket(git).

• Languages worked on: JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML, SCSS and CSS An MoU was signed between the Institute of Cost and Management Accountants of Pakistan (ICMA Pakistan) and Befiler Pvt Ltd to promote job opportunities and provide professional training to ICMA Pakistan students.300,000 individuals and SMEs are now using Befiler to deal their matter of tax.
